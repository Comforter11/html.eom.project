My Story.

My son is my inspiration as I my number one model,
he is also the man behind the name "Prince M" and I am Comforter M
Prince M Photography is a baby, born in 2020, only as a part time job,
but because of the zeal in me I had to give this baby more attention.
In this doing, I fell deeper inlove with photography and I’ve been living my
dream being a full time photographer ever since,
helping families keep memories for generations to come is my pleasure.
I would describe my style as vibrant, timeless & heartfelt.

I'm more focused on maternity, newborn, cake smashes and fashion. I also have a great passion for family photography!

I’m very fond of kids and I love reading books too. I have three brothers who are still in school.
My mom is a sales-lady in a salon. I got married on the 5th of May 2019 to Steven M, my best friend and soul mate.
I am a big believer in Christ and how He’s provided for me, and keeps providing continuously.
I enjoy being outside on sunny days, spending time with the people that matter most to me
but I love the rainy days yet prefer cold ones.
